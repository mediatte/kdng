Product Information

This is an application for students to learn critical reading.

Screenshot

Key Features

  • Paraphrasing a given article into easier words
  • Pinpoint the topic, keywords, or title of a given article
  • Providing Cloze tests and multiple-choice questions along with its model answer
  • Suggesting translations, vocabulary lists
  • Powerful chatting service about a given article
